Chapter 9: Global Standards – Establishing Ethical and Legal Frameworks

Page 3 – Global Standards

Global standards ensure that expert‑level AI practice aligns with ethical, cultural, and legal frameworks worldwide. Establishing these standards fosters trust, accountability, and collaboration across nations and industries.

Why Global Standards Matter

  • Consistency – Provides unified guidelines for AI adoption worldwide.
  • Fairness – Promotes equitable access and prevents exploitation.
