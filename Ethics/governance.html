Chapter 6: Governance – Establishing Ethical Oversight

Page 6 – Governance

Governance provides the structures and policies that ensure AI systems are used responsibly. Effective governance balances innovation with ethical oversight, protecting both organizations and society.

Why Governance Matters

  • Oversight – Establishes checks and balances for AI use.
  • Compliance – Aligns AI practices with laws and regulations.
  • Ethics – Ensures moral principles guide AI deployment.
  • Risk Management – Reduces potential harms through structured policies.

Best Practices

  • Create governance boards to oversee AI projects.
  • Develop clear policies for ethical AI use.
  • Engage diverse stakeholders in governance decisions.
  • Review governance frameworks regularly to adapt to new challenges.

Practical Exercise

  • Draft a governance policy for one AI workflow.
  • Identify stakeholders who should be involved in oversight.
  • Define escalation procedures for ethical concerns.
  • Document governance measures for transparency.

By establishing governance, expert users ensure that AI systems are managed responsibly, balancing innovation with accountability and ethical oversight.
