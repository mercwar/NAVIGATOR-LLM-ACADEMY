Chapter 6: Sustainability – Long‑Term Ethical AI

Page 9 – Sustainability

Sustainability ensures that AI systems remain ethical, efficient, and beneficial over the long term. It emphasizes environmental, social, and organizational responsibility in AI development and deployment.

Why Sustainability Matters

  • Environmental Impact – Reducing energy consumption and carbon footprint.
  • Social Responsibility – Ensuring AI benefits communities equitably.
  • Organizational Longevity – Building systems that adapt and endure.
  • Global Trust – Sustainable practices foster worldwide confidence in AI.

Best Practices

  • Optimize models for energy efficiency.
  • Use renewable energy sources for training and deployment.
  • Design workflows that minimize waste and redundancy.
  • Engage in corporate social responsibility initiatives.

Practical Exercise

  • Review one AI workflow you use.
  • Estimate its environmental and social impact.
  • Propose changes to make it more sustainable.
  • Document sustainability improvements for accountability.

By embedding sustainability, expert users ensure that AI systems contribute positively to society and the environment, maintaining ethical value for generations to come.
