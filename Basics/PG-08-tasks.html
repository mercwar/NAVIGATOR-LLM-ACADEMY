Chapter 1: What Is It Good For – Computer Tasks Made Easy

Page 8 – Computer Tasks Made Easy

Large Language Models (LLMs) simplify everyday computer tasks. Instead of memorizing commands or searching through menus, users can describe what they want in plain language and let the model handle the details.

Common tasks made easier by LLMs include:

  • Email drafting – Write professional messages quickly.
  • Document editing – Summarize, reformat, or improve clarity.
  • Spreadsheet help – Generate formulas or explain data.
  • Automation – Create scripts or workflows without coding expertise.

These capabilities reduce friction for novice users and increase productivity for professionals. By offloading repetitive or complex tasks to an LLM, you can focus on creativity and decision‑making.

Practical Exercise

  • Ask your LLM to draft a polite email requesting information.
  • Paste a paragraph of text and request a summary.
  • Provide a simple math problem and see how the LLM explains the steps.
  • Try asking for a spreadsheet formula (e.g., “sum values in column A”).

By practicing these tasks, you’ll see how LLMs can serve as everyday assistants, making computing more accessible and efficient.
