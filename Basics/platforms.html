Chapter 1: Handshake with LLMs – Finding Platforms

Page 5 – Platforms

To begin using Large Language Models (LLMs), you need to know where to find them. Several platforms provide access to these systems, ranging from free demos to professional tools integrated into everyday applications.

Popular platforms include:

  • Microsoft Copilot – integrated into Office apps and Windows.
  • ChatGPT – a widely used conversational AI by OpenAI.
  • Hugging Face – a hub for open‑source models and demos.
  • OpenAI Playground – an interactive environment for testing prompts.

Each platform has its own strengths. Some focus on accessibility and ease of use, while others emphasize customization and developer tools. Beginners should start with platforms that offer simple interfaces and free access.

Practical Exercise

  • Visit one of the platforms listed above.
  • Create a free account if required.
  • Type a simple question into the interface (e.g., “What is a Large Language Model?”).
  • Observe how the system responds and compare across platforms.

By exploring multiple platforms, you’ll gain a sense of how LLMs differ in style, speed, and capabilities. This handshake is your first step toward deeper learning.
