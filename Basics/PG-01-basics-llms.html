Chapter 1: Basics – Learning More About LLMs

Introduction

Large Language Models (LLMs) are advanced artificial intelligence systems trained on massive amounts of text data. They are capable of generating, summarizing, and explaining information in natural language, making them useful for everyday computer users.

In this chapter, we will explore the foundations of LLMs, starting with their history, definitions, and importance. Each page builds on the previous one, guiding novice users step by step toward understanding how these systems work and how they can be applied.

By the end of Chapter 1, you will have a clear understanding of what LLMs are, why they matter, and how you can begin using them in your daily life.
