Chapter 1: LLMs Everywhere – Joe Tron’s Future Vision

Page 9 – Future Vision

Large Language Models (LLMs) are rapidly becoming embedded in every aspect of computing. From personal devices to enterprise systems, they are transforming how humans interact with technology.

Joe Tron’s vision of the future imagines LLMs as universal companions — integrated into operating systems, browsers, and applications. Instead of searching, clicking, or coding, users will simply describe what they want, and the LLM will orchestrate the task.

This future includes multimodal systems that combine text, images, audio, and video. Distributed architectures will allow LLMs to run across networks, balancing performance and privacy. Everyday tasks will be streamlined, and complex workflows will be simplified.

Practical Exercise

  • Imagine how your daily routine could change if every app had an LLM built in.
  • Write down three tasks you perform regularly that could be automated.
  • Consider how multimodal input (voice, image, text) might improve those tasks.

By envisioning the future of LLMs, you’ll better understand their potential impact and prepare yourself to adapt to the next wave of computing innovation.
