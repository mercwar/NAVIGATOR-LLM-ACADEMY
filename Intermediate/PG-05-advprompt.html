Chapter 2: Advanced Prompt Techniques

Page 5 – Advanced Prompting

Advanced prompt techniques allow intermediate users to push Large Language Models (LLMs) beyond basic responses. These methods involve structuring prompts with precision, layering instructions, and leveraging role‑based guidance.

Techniques

  • Role Assignment – Ask the model to act as a teacher, coder, or analyst.
  • Step‑by‑Step Instructions – Break tasks into ordered steps for clarity.
  • Few‑Shot Examples – Provide sample inputs and outputs to guide responses.
  • Formatting Control – Specify output style (tables, lists, JSON, etc.).
  • Multi‑Prompt Chaining – Use sequential prompts to refine results.

These techniques increase accuracy and creativity. They also help reduce common issues like vague answers or hallucinations by anchoring the model’s behavior.

Practical Exercise

  • Ask your LLM: “Act as a career coach. Suggest three strategies for improving productivity.”
  • Provide a few‑shot example: “Input: Write a haiku about the ocean. Output: Gentle waves whisper.”
  • Request structured output: “List five pros and cons of remote work in a table.”

By mastering advanced prompt techniques, you’ll gain control over the model’s output and unlock its full potential for complex tasks.
